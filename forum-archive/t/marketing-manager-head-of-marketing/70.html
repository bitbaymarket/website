You will need:

• To be a marketing professional with strong brand and advertising leadership experience Ideally with a mixture of B2B, B2C or C2C experience.
• The ability to translate customer insight into clear and compelling customer messages and communication
• The ability to join up multiple strands of activity to land in the market at the same time for maximum impact in terms of impact, influence, and ultimately sales
• To be highly commercially aware

• Strong commercial and numerical analytical skills
