How to rescan within the BitBay QT Wallet (PC)

Get Started Guides
tradersnow 2020-03-31 20:57:02 UTC #1

Initiating a “rescan” will allow your wallet to update your address’s history of inputs, possibly correcting any issues with your total coin balance.

PC:

Follow these steps to initiate a “rescan” on PC:

1. Right click the QT app and select “Create shortcut”.

2. Drag the newly created shortcut to your Desktop (to keep separate from parent app)

3. Right click the new shortcut and click on “Properties”.

4. Go to the “Shortcut” tab and find the “Target” box.

Go to the Shortcut tab and then to Target.png395x543 63.9 KB

5. If the last part of the text within the “Target” box shows: “bitbay-wallet-qt-exe” , then you may proceed to the next step.

If the last part of Target looks like this you can go to the next step.png362x528 20.3 KB

6. Click at the end of the text, and add a space with “-rescan” (without quotations). Then click “ok”.

Go to the end of the text and add a space and then -rescan - then you can press ok.png360x526 16 KB

7. Now use the shortcut to open the QT Wallet App. After opening the QT and waiting for the blocks to load, you will see “rescanning”. This can take some time (up to an hour or two) depending on the number of inputs your address has.

After first loading the blocks it should say rescanning and youre ok.jpg744x407 136 KB

Once it has finished rescanning, your wallet balance should be up to date.
