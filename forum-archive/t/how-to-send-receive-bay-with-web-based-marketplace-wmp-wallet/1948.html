How to send & receive BAY with Web-based Marketplace (WMP) wallet

How to receive BAY

  1. From the “Account” page, click the “Addresses” tab on the left side of the page. Here, you will see your “Primary Address” (aka public key), which can be copied and pasted for sending funds to the wallet.

    Alternatively, you can also click the “Balance” icon in the upper left corner of any page, and then click “Copy address”.
