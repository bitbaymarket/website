Have been curious about the possibilities of contract assignments with BitBay’s DDE?

For example: a merchant goes into contract with a potential seller, if merchant cannot purchase outright or come to terms with seller, the merchant then assigns that contract to someone in their pool of other contacts/buyers who actually can meet the terms.
