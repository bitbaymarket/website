So far, only the QT wallet and the Markets Client have been updated to support the dynamic peg. The web wallet is next on the list of items to update. It is currently impossible to send your coins directly to the exchange, because they are on a different fork of the BitBay blockchain.

To get your coins out of your web/mobile wallet, you must first import your private key into the QT Wallet or Markets Client. Then you can send them to the exchange.
