First, please follow these steps to identify which chain you are on:

1. Open the Markets Client, and click on “Settings” -> “Debug Console”.

2. Then, within the console, type:

text=str(BLK.getblock("e89164a165df952a3bc4b80d9ea33c3be47baa2406bbd7c9bd04eb91bd11e23a"))

3. Then click “Execute code”. If you see the block information, then you are on the correct chain:

If your results are “block not found”, then you are on the wrong chain, please follow the instructions in this article to update your client:
